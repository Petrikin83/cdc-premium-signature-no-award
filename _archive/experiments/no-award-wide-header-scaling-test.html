No-Award Wide Header Scaling Test

Goal: Find a no-award header design that makes Gmail app on iPhone treat the signature as a full 580px block and apply proportional scale-to-fit — rather than content-reflow (which causes address to wrap and 2×2 grid to shift).
Hypothesis: The award <img width="184"> forced Gmail app to see a hard 580px content width. Without it, Gmail collapses the empty right column and reflows at ~375px, causing layout instability.
